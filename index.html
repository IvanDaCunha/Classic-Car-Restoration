Welcome to Jack Jeston's Restorations

Jeston with his Classical Car

Jack Jeston’s passion for classic cars began at a young age, growing up in a small town where his grandfather, a retired mechanic, introduced him to the world of vintage vehicles. The garage was always filled with the distinct sounds of engines, the smell of oil, and the hum of tools at work, creating a magical environment that sparked Jack's curiosity. His grandfather allowed him to assist with small tasks, teaching him the intricate details of car maintenance and repair. As Jack grew older, his fascination with these timeless machines deepened, and he began restoring abandoned cars, quickly gaining a reputation for his meticulous craftsmanship and love for preserving automotive history.Eventually, Jack turned his passion into a business, opening his own classic car restoration shop. Known for his attention to detail and dedication to authenticity, he quickly became a trusted name among car enthusiasts. His workshop was filled with vehicles in various stages of restoration, from stripped-down frames to fully restored masterpieces. Jack’s pride and joy was a 1967 Mustang that he had spent years perfecting. For him, working on classic cars wasn’t just a job—it was a way of life, a celebration of automotive history, and a means of connecting to the past while ensuring these iconic machines would live on for future generations to admire and enjoy.
